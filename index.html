About Me

Hello, my name is Filipe Cancellier da Costa.

I was born and raised in Brazil, more specifically in the city of Urussanga, Santa Catarina. I consider myself a good and a simple person, in my free time you will probably find me watching a good movie, playing some game on my laptop, exploring the city (because London is awesome) or studying coding.

My career is summarized in engineering jobs. I got graduate in Civil Engineering in 2017, when I started working as a Civil Engineer in a city close to my hometown. Although most of my professionals skill are turned to this field, I felt that something wasn't right something was missing, I wasn't totally happy being a Civil Engineer, so I decided to challenge myself and try learn something new.

I always enjoyed computer, games and technology so I thought "Why not try coding?", and so I did. I started enrolling in some free online programming courses and ended up starting with Python. I was enjoying learning Python but one day, my cousin, who is a front-end engineer, showed me a little bit about web development. So I decided to try some Javascript, just to see what it would be like. After I wrote my first “Hello World” in Javascript, I really fell in love with all this, the freedom and limitless creative that it gives to you, and you can see every line of code that you wrote working on the screen.

Since then, I have been teaching myself coding for web ( JS, CSS , HTML ), through some online courses such as Udemy, Udacity, RocketSeat and some websites to challenge your algorithms skills such as codewars and hackerrank. However, is a bit hard to try learn everything by yourself. There is indeed tons of material on internet, but you have to know what to focus on and what not. I've been studying for over an year and I am really enjoying each new step each new feature that I learn every day and I really want to take this to a new level.

Joining FoundersAndCoders would be the perfect opportunity to learn in a real way how to work in groups and how improve my coding skills to a professional level. I would love to be part of this community, discuss with others about programming and about new ideas to solve their problems, I am really looking forward to being part of this program and learn as much as I can!
