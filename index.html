Hello, my name is Filipe Cancellier da Costa.

I have a graduation in Civil Engineering and I'm originally from Brazil.

My career is resumed to engineering jobs, so all my professionals skill are turned to this area, but I was felling that something was missing, I wasn't totally happy in Civil Engineer area, so I decided to try a new thing. I always enjoyed computer, games and technology so I thought "Why not try codding?", and so I did.

I started my studies in codding in 2018, and at that time I started studying Python and so my first "Hello World" was done in Python. The codding was nice, all the logic and the algorithms, I was really enjoying that, but I wasn't felling good about the language, I mean Python is an amazing language, but for a start, you would like to see things happening on screen, and not see only numbers.
So I had a conversation with my cousin, that is a Front-End Engineer, and he showed me HTML, CSS and Javascript, he said that maybe I would like it more than python. After that day I turned all my studies routine to learn web-development.
It's so nice to learn web, because you can see everything that you code in front of you and can see how much do you progress every single day.

I am studying every day, but I don't fell that I already have enough for a job in web area, so when I heard about Founders and Coders I thought that this could be the perfect opportunity for me to learn more and get the confidence to start a career web-development. Founders and Coders seems to be a great community, fulled with people who want to learn and who wants to help, it really look like a nice place be your first step in your programming career. And thats why I'm looking forward to be part of this program, and learn as much as I can!
